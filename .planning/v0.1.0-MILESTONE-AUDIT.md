### 1. CONV-07 Signal Optimization Unwired (SPEC-07, IMPL-02)

### 2. CONV-08 PURE Annotations Not Injected (SPEC-08, IMPL-02, IMPL-05)

`_needs_pure` at `transform.rs:1312` is computed and discarded. No `/*#__PURE__*/` comment injected on `componentQrl()` calls in Segment/Inline/Single strategies. This is a dominant cause of the 200/201 root module mismatches in the parity report.

### 3. Dual Pipeline Divergence

`lib.rs::transform_code` (production) and `transform.rs::transform_code` (test-only) have different stage orderings. `filter_ctx_names` pre-pass is absent from the production pipeline.
